# CVSS (Common Vulnerability Scoring System)

CVSS (Common Vulnerability Scoring System) is an open framework for rating the severity of security vulnerabilities on a 0 to 10 scale using Base, Temporal, and Environmental metric groups. It captures characteristics such as attack vector, attack complexity, privileges required, and impact to confidentiality, integrity, and availability to produce a numeric score and severity label. It is widely used to prioritize remediation and is commonly applied to CVE entries.
