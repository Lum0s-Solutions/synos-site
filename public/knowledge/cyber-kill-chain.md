# Cyber Kill Chain

The Cyber Kill Chain is a model developed by Lockheed Martin that describes the stages of a cyber intrusion: Reconnaissance, Weaponization, Delivery, Exploitation, Installation, Command and Control, and Actions on Objectives. Defenders use it to understand attacker progression and to identify opportunities to detect and disrupt an attack at each phase. It is a foundational framework in threat-informed defense.
