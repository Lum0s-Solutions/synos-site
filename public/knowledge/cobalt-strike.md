# Cobalt Strike

> [Defensive analysis only.]

---
title: Cobalt Strike
tags: [concept, cybersecurity, tool]
---

Cobalt Strike is a commercial adversary-simulation and red-team platform providing a command-and-control (C2) framework with "Beacon" implants for post-exploitation, lateral movement, and covert communication. It models advanced-threat tradecraft, including malleable C2 profiles, and is widely used by red teams while its cracked versions are frequently abused by real-world attackers. It is a common focus of threat detection and hunting efforts.
