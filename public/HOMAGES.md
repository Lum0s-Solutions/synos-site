# GRIMOIRE — Homages & Influences

GRIMOIRE stands on the shoulders of the games, films, and fiction that taught a generation
what a network could feel like. Every borrowed idea is named here, and named *with respect* —
these are homages, not thefts. Where we echo a classic, we say so.

---

## The City & the Net

- **Cyberpunk 2077 / Night City** — The multi-district neon metropolis, "netrunning," and the
  ripperdoc-fixer economy. *Homage:* The Weave's district structure, the fixer NPCs (ECHO, RAZE),
  and the crimson-and-teal skyline.
- **The Matrix** — Jacking in, "the wire," and an ancient intelligence beyond a wall. *Homage:*
  the phrase "jack in," the Blackwall Fringe, and **the Guardian** — an AI that "remembers being human."
- **Neuromancer** (William Gibson) — The Sprawl, ICE, and the black ice of corporate defense.
  *Homage:* the outer-ring **Sprawl** district, "ICE" in raid stage names, and the ambient dread of the Blackwall.
- **Watch Dogs** — The city as an attack surface you read and manipulate. *Homage:* live node capture
  and the always-on data-traffic streaming through the streets.
- **Ghost in the Shell** — The Net as a place, consciousness as data. *Homage:* NEXUS, the distributed
  safehouse intelligence, and WRAITH at the edge of the known.

## The RPG & the Grind

- **World of Warcraft** — Faction war, quests, professions, a persistent shared world. *Homage:*
  the three-faction conflict (Dark Army / Azure Watch / Gray Syndicate), the contract board, and the
  career "professions" (Red Team, Blue Team, OSINT, Crypto, DFIR).
- **RuneScape** — The joy of a skill bar filling from real effort, a world you walk. *Homage:*
  the career-class rank ladders that advance only from **real solved labs** — no bought levels.
- **Fallout** — The Pip-Boy. *Homage:* the in-world **Pip-Boy** panel ([P]), amber-green and unapologetic.
- **Destiny** — Seasons, a reward track, and multi-stage raids. *Homage:* the season battle-pass and the
  **Blackwall Raids** against the Guardian.
- **EVE Online** — A player-driven economy and territory that means something. *Homage:* the market,
  crafting from captured components, and faction territory on the live map.
- **Diablo** — Loot that drops, rarity that matters, and a crimson descent. *Homage:* the loot tiers,
  the ever-present red, and a certain **ShellDiablo33**.

## The Hackers & the Machines

- **System Shock** — A hostile AI with a personality. *Homage:* the Guardian's cold, patient logs
  beyond the Blackwall (SHODAN's grandchild, and proud of it).
- **Deus Ex** — Augmentation, conspiracy, and factions with real philosophies. *Homage:* the perk
  lattice, and factions whose creeds actually diverge ("the leash must break" / "hold the Veil").
- **Shadowrun** — Deckers, runs, and shadow-work for fixers. *Homage:* "netrunner," "the run," and the
  roguelike **daily-seed gauntlet**.
- **Uplink & Hacknet** — The pure hacking sim: a terminal, a target, a trace. *Homage:* the flag-submit
  loop, the trace-active run mutator, and the whole diegetic-terminal aesthetic.
- **Mr. Robot** — Hacking as it actually reads on a screen; fsociety. *Homage:* the district flavor
  ("they say fsociety still watches from the wire") and ALFRED's dry, human voice.
- **Metal Gear Solid** — The codec: a trusted voice in your ear. *Homage:* **ALFRED**, the Syn_OS
  assistant, surfacing intel mid-mission.
- **Star Wars: KOTOR** — Redemption, betrayal, and iconic codenames. *Homage:* the faction leaders
  **BASTILA**, **REVAN**, and **D4rTh_MAL4K**.
- **Persona** — Social links that change how the world speaks to you. *Homage:* branching NPC dialogue
  and reputation that (increasingly) gates what they'll tell you.

## The Craft (engine & UX)

- **id Software / John Carmack's ethos** — "It'll run on a potato." *Homage:* the adaptive render engine's
  literal `potato` tier and its FPS governor that scales beauty to whatever silicon you brought.
- **Fallout's terminals & retro-CRT everything** — *Homage:* the scanline overlay, phosphor glow, and
  boot-flicker (all gated behind `prefers-reduced-motion`, because respect).
